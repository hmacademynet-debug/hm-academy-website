What CBSE classes does HM Academy cover?
HM Academy offers 1:1 CBSE online tuition for Classes 6 to 10, covering Maths, Science, Social Studies, and English. We also provide skill-based summer courses for students.
Are HM Academy classes online or offline?
All classes are 100% online. Our CBSE online tuition is conducted via live video sessions, allowing students across India to learn from the comfort of home.
How does 1:1 CBSE online tuition work?
In our 1:1 online tuition, each student gets individual attention from the mentor. Sessions are customised based on the student’s learning level, ensuring better understanding and faster improvement.
